Own story

Weaving sustainability and identity into every stitch.
Twist Your Noodles was born from the meeting of two worlds: Asian Culture, full of creativity and concepts, and a strong passion for crochet. Our products celebrate the value of handmade pieces, where slow, thoughtful crafting meets creativity and self-expression.

The Lab: The Noodle Journey

A look behind the scenes: quality, care, and the moment the twisted yarn becomes yours.

STEP 1

We only use sustainable materials. Each yarn is selected for its quality, texture and minimal environmental impact.

STEP 2

The creation process is slow. Every stitch is placed with precision, ensuring the unique twist of our artistic vision.

STEP 3

The finished piece is now ready to leave the lab and become a part of your story, outfit and style.
